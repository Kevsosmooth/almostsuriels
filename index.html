Dress Code

Honestly, we couldn't decide with a dress code for a long time, because all our friends are creative gurus, and we did not want to tell you what to wear on our terms. Nevertheless, the wedding will be in a rustic style, so please pay attention to our recommendations below.

Ladies

We ask our lovely ladies to wear cocktail or evening dresses. The color palette for formal or semi-formal attire is recommended. Please try to avoid wearing white, ivory, or cream -- those shades are reserved for the bride. Most importantly, wear something that makes you feel beautiful and lets you dance the night away in comfort.

Gentlemen

Guys, please wear suits, blazers, or dress pants. A tie is optional but always a nice touch. We recommend neutral or dark tones -- navy, charcoal, black, or earth tones are all great options. Feel free to add a pop of color with your pocket square or tie. The key is to look sharp and feel comfortable enough to hit the dance floor.

ZWF Miami

16225 SW 172nd Ave, Miami, FL 33187

If you're looking to get up close and personal with some wild animals, we HIGHLY recommend you check this place out! We held a monkey, lemur and an owl!

Monkey Jungle

14805 SW 216th St, Miami, FL 33170

A unique wildlife park where the monkeys roam free and you walk through their habitat. A fun and unforgettable experience for animal lovers.

Homestead Bayfront Park

9698 SW 328th St, Homestead, FL 33033

A beautiful waterfront park perfect for relaxing, kayaking, or enjoying a stunning sunrise. Great spot to unwind before or after the wedding festivities.

Magic City Casino

450 NW 37th Ave, Miami, FL 33125

Feeling lucky? Head over to Magic City Casino for some gaming, live entertainment, and great food. A fun night out while you're in town.

Everglades National Park

305 Lincoln Rd, Miami Beach, FL 33139

Explore the breathtaking Everglades with an airboat ride or nature walk. See alligators, birds, and the vast beauty of Florida's iconic wetlands.

The Falls

8888 SW 136th St, Miami, FL 33176

An open-air shopping mall with beautiful landscaping, waterfalls, and a great selection of stores and restaurants. Perfect for a relaxing afternoon outing.

Florida Keys Outlet Marketplace

250 E Palm Dr, Florida City, FL 33034

Grab some deals at this outlet mall on your way to or from the Keys. Great brands at discount prices -- a perfect pit stop for bargain hunters.
