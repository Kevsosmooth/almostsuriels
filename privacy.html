When you use our RSVP form, we may collect the following information:

The information you provide is used solely for the purpose of planning our wedding. This includes:

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data may be processed by the third-party services we use to operate this website (such as our hosting provider and form processor), but only to the extent necessary to provide those services.

Cookies

This website may use essential cookies to remember your site access credentials. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics services that collect personal data.

Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete any personal information you have provided to us at any time by contacting us directly.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, please reach out to us directly through the contact information provided on our wedding invitations.
